We all have "weird" hobbies and likes, but these are my weird likings. And I thought if I were to make websites, at least one of them should be about my weirdness but also let it be a space for anyone else who wants to express their own weirdness. I am definetly against anyone who looks down on others just because of their diversities, therefore there will not be any toleration for anyone like that here.It is a safe space here. Anyway, here are some of weird hobbies:

    1. READING:I like all sorts of books and though some may think reading is about serious all the time but I like to indulge in more than one genre.My favorites are fantasy and sci-fi.
    2. WRITING:I know it is kinda cliche but I like to write especially when I am feeling strong emotions and it really helped me over the years as I was never one to express what I would be feeling.My favorite is poetry but even if I have written quite a bit but I do not like to show it often.

Anyway back to being weird, I said something about writing Poetry,here are some of them just a glimpse of the recent ones:

  • NIGHTMARES TO REMEMBER:
    It was like a nightmare,
    Indeed like a horrible dream but one that leaves us with a lesson,
    One that should teach the world of today and tomorrow the consequences of the likes of “little boy” and “fat man”,
    One that should leave us all wanting to always find nonviolent ways to strive for peace,
    So, today as the world comes together to remember, let us also make a promise in memory of those who passed that their lives will not be in vain,
    Though nightmares are forgotten, but this is reality that should never be forgotten.
    Together for peace
    Heiwanotameni
  • THE UNCENSORED LIFE:
    I met an old man,
    The years had passed so fast and so did his youth,
    Grief and trauma being his best friends till he breathes his last,
    Having been robbed of his youth was not his concern but anger of how much he sacrificed to be happy in his last days,
    No grandchild, no child and no wife, only a shattering silence that makes him regret not dying in the wars he fought,
    Hope he had for a better future burned to ashes as he was wise enough to see another brewing which thankfully he would not be there to see as it would bring him further sorrow.

I know you probably see a recurring theme but I was never one to write about smushy stuff, I guess I am not cut out for such.Anyway there are other reasons why I write, it is more of an inspiration really, when I hear stories of what others passed through makes me think how I would feel if i were in their shoes.
